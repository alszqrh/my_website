Hidden Gems Restaurant in Malaysia



List of Best Restaurant in Each States

  • Kedah: Known for its traditional Malay cuisine like Nasi Kandar, a flavorful rice dish with various curries.
  • Perlis: Offers delicious seafood dishes due to its coastal location, such as grilled fish and prawn noodles.
  • Pulau Pinang: Famous for its street food scene, especially dishes like Char Kway Teow (stir-fried noodles) and Penang Laksa.
  • Perak: Enjoy local favorites like Ipoh Hor Fun (flat rice noodles) and Chicken Rice, which are must-try dishes.
  • Kelantan: Explore traditional Kelantanese cuisine with specialties like Nasi Kerabu (blue rice) and Ayam Percik (grilled chicken in spicy coconut sauce).
  • Terengganu: Savour fresh seafood dishes like Nasi Dagang (rice cooked in coconut milk) and Keropok Lekor (fish crackers).
  • Pahang: Indulge in local delights such as Patin Tempoyak (catfish in fermented durian paste) and Ikan Bakar (grilled fish).
  • Hulu Selangor, Selangor: Try local favorites like Asam Pedas (sour and spicy fish stew) and Satay, a popular Malaysian skewered meat dish.

SEMPORNA, SABAH!




Landed On Tawau Airport


When I landed at Tawau Airport, the atmosphere was buzzing with excitement. After stepping off the plane, I felt the warm tropical air enveloping me. The airport had a laid-back vibe, typical of smaller regional airports, with friendly staff and a few local shops. As I waited for the car rental owner, I took in the surroundings, observing other travelers and the unique decor that hinted at the beauty of Sabah. Once me and my family met up with the car rental owner, the drive to Semporna was filled with anticipation. The scenic route offered glimpses of lush greenery and maybe even some local villages, adding to the adventure before reaching my hotel.




Furthermore, we arrived at MD Mutiara Hotel, I was warmly greeted by the friendly staff, who welcomed me with genuine smiles that instantly made me feel at home. The hotel exuded a cozy and inviting atmosphere, beautifully complemented by its modern design that blended comfort with style. After settling in and taking a moment to appreciate my surroundings, I made my way to the restaurant, Cfu' Tomyam Seafood Semporna, where I was excited to indulge in some delicious local cuisine, eager to experience the flavors of the region and enjoy a delightful meal.
